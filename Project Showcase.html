ECOBRICKS FOR ZERO WASTE

Ecobricks are an innovative approach to addressing plastic waste, aiming to promote zero waste and environmental sustainability. They involve filling plastic bottles with non-biodegradable waste, such as single-use plastics, to create durable, reusable building blocks for construction projects, furniture, or insulation. This practice not only helps manage plastic waste but also raises awareness about reducing, reusing, and recycling.
